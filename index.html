Our Values

KOBU is a business that believes that what we have to offer goes beyond a certain good or service. We primarily aim to create value not only economically, but emotionally and spiritually for both our constituents and our beneficiaries. Because of this, KOBU is a deeply value-centric organization. We aim to foster a culture of excellence both in ourselves and others by taking actions that are congruent with certain values.

Community and Leadership

First and foremost, KOBU is a believer in the power of the individual. We believe a single person can change the world for good or ill, and when you put these individuals together, you end up with something even more powerful. Community, to us, is a term that ultimately refers to people. A community can be held together by emotion, a shared set of beliefs, or a common purpose. We aim not only to help those communities in need, but to introduce them to the tools they need to help themselves.

We aim not just to lead others, but to help people discover their ability for leadership. Programs such as our project accelerator are geared towards helping people who have the passion to start helping their community find the talent and leadership skills inside of themselves to execute it. In this way KOBU acts not only as a helping hand, but also as a catalyst to galvanize action in communities through an individual.
